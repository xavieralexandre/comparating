Comparating

Better rating through comparison

Comparating is a project aimed at testing the practicability of rating things by comparing them by pairs.

It's a well-known idea (Elo Rating System, Facemash, Polar...) and I would like to try it on different use-cases to see if it is well suited to certain kind of activities.

In particular, I'm often confronted to the situation when a group of people want to agree on a choice. Be it which ice-cream flavors to buy, which user-stories to be completed in priority, which film to watch, or which restaurant where to eat.

A tool where you could choose or upload your set of things to rate, have everyone vote, and visualize the results to reach a consensus.
